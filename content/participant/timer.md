# Timer

When the facilitator runs a countdown, it appears in the bar at the bottom of
your screen and follows you from tool to tool. It's the shared clock for the
current activity — when it reaches zero, the group moves on.

## Things you can do

- **Glance at it** — that's its job. No action needed.
- **Tap it** to fill your screen with the countdown — handy if your phone is
  propped up where others can see it, or you just want the time large. Tap
  again (or press Esc) to go back; the fullscreen view is only on your device.

The timer is run by the facilitator — starting, pausing and resetting are
theirs.
