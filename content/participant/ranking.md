# Ranking

Put the items in your order of preference. Everyone's orders combine into a
group average.

## How to take part

- Give each item a position — your 1st choice, 2nd, and so on.
- Your ranking is yours; you don't have to agree with anyone else.
- The group's average order updates as people rank.

## Tips

- Rank on the criterion the facilitator set (impact, feasibility, fit…).
- Where the group agrees, items settle quickly; where it disagrees, you'll see
  it in the colours — that disagreement is useful, not a problem.
