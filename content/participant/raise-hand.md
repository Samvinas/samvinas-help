# Raise Hand

Want to say something without talking over anyone? Tap the ✋ in the bar at
the bottom of your screen. You join a queue the facilitator can see, in order
— they'll come to you.

- Tap once to raise your hand; tap again to lower it if the moment passes.
- Your place in the queue is kept while you carry on using whatever tool is
  active.
- When the facilitator calls on you, your hand is cleared automatically.
