# Voting

You have a set number of stars — spend them on the ideas you most want to move
forward.

## How to take part

- Tap the star on an item to give it a vote; tap again to take it back.
- You can see how many votes you have left at the top.
- Items rise as they gather votes, so the group's favourites surface live.

## Making your votes count

- Vote for what genuinely excites you or best meets the group's criteria — not
  just your own idea.
- Spread your votes or concentrate them; it's your call.
- There are no wrong votes. This is a quick read of the room, not the final
  decision.
