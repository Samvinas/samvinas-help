# Stakeholder Map

Place each person or group on two axes — by default, how much **influence** they
have and how **interested** they are.

## How to take part

- For each stakeholder, set their position on both axes.
- The map shows everyone's placements together.

## Tips

- Be honest about influence and interest — the map is only useful if it reflects
  reality.
- Where the group places someone very differently, that's worth a conversation.
