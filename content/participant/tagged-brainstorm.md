# Tagged Brainstorming

This is a brainstorm with labelled buckets — instead of one long list, your
ideas go straight into the category they belong to (for example *Helps* and
*Hinders*).

## How to take part

- Tap a coloured bucket bar to open it. Inside you'll find a box to add ideas
  and the list of what everyone has posted there, newest first.
- Add your idea to whichever bucket fits. Not sure? Pick the nearest one —
  ideas can be re-filed later, by anyone, using the selector on the item.
- Keep moving between buckets: reading one category often sparks ideas for
  another.

## The usual brainstorm rules still apply

- **Quantity first** — a rough idea in the right bucket now beats a polished
  one after the moment has passed.
- **Defer judgement** — no idea gets criticised at this stage.
- **Build on others** — reply to what you see with a "yes, and…" idea of your
  own.
