# Elaboration

Take a chosen idea and develop it — add detail, reasoning, and sketches.

## How to take part

- Pick an item and open it to elaborate.
- Write in Markdown; use `/sketch` for a quick drawing.
- You can come back and edit your elaboration later.

## Tips

- This is where a promising idea becomes a real proposal — be specific.
- Stuck starting? Describe the idea as if it's already running: *"What I see
  us doing is…"* — the present tense forces the detail out.
