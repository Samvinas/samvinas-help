# ALoU

ALoU helps the group look at an idea from three angles and then strengthen it.

For each idea you can add to three sections:

- **Advantages** — what is genuinely good about this idea.
- **Limitations** — what worries you, written as a question so it can be
  answered, e.g. "How might we…?"
- **Unique Features** — what is distinctive or original about it.

Under a limitation, add an **Overcome**: a concrete way to answer that particular
limitation. Everyone sees everyone's contributions, so build on what others add.

The aim isn't to pick the idea apart — it's to understand it fully and make it
stronger.
