# Rich Brainstorm

Like Brainstorm, but you can format your ideas and even sketch them.

## How to take part

- Type your idea; use simple Markdown for emphasis, lists, and headings.
- Type `/sketch` to open a drawing canvas for a quick diagram, then finish to
  attach it.
- Tap any idea to see it full size.

## Tips

- A rough sketch often says more than a paragraph — don't aim for polish.
- Same spirit as Brainstorm: quantity first, judgement later.
