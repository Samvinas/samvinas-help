# Backcasting

The group is working backwards from a **desired future** to today, placing the
steps that need to happen onto a rough timeline.

- The desired future is shown at the top as the anchor.
- Each step has a dropdown. Give it a number from **0 to 9** — **0** is the
  earliest (nearest to now) and **9** is right before the future state. Choose
  **"–"** to leave a step unplaced for now.
- You're all working on the same board, so anyone can place or move a step, and
  everyone sees the changes live.

This is about the rough *order* of things, not exact dates — a shared sense of
what has to happen when.
