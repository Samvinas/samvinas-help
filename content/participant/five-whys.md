# Five Whys

Get past symptoms to a real cause by asking "why?" — and then "why?" again.

## How to take part

- Start from the problem and add a **why** — the reason behind it.
- Then ask why *that* happens, and keep drilling. Five is a guide, not a rule.
- Together you're building a tree of causes.

## Tips

- Aim for honest causes, not blame.
- If a branch stops being useful, back up and try a different "why."
