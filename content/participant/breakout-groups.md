# Breakout Groups

Work in a small group with its own shared space, then rejoin the whole room.

## How to take part

- Pick a group to join.
- Inside, your group has its own tools and results — work together there.
- The facilitator may bring your group's output back to the main room.

## Tips

- Small groups mean everyone gets airtime — use it.
- Check your group's instructions (the **?** or an Instructions page) if you're
  unsure what to do.
