# Tagging

Sort items into the categories the facilitator has set up.

## How to take part

- For each item, choose a category from the dropdown.
- You'll see how the group is tagging things, and where there's consensus.

## Seeing the results

The **Results** tab shows how the group is tagging each item and where consensus
is forming. Try to **make your own calls first** before studying it — deciding
for yourself keeps your read independent, and the disagreements are exactly what
makes the group's tagging worth discussing.

## Tips

- Go with your judgement; where people tag an item differently, that difference
  is informative.
