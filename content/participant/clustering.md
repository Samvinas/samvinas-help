# Clustering

Group ideas that belong together and give each group a name. It turns a long
list into a handful of themes.

## How to take part

- Tap to select related items, then group them.
- Name each group so its meaning is clear.
- Leave items ungrouped if they don't fit anywhere — that's fine.

## Tips

- There's no single right grouping; look for patterns that are useful to the
  group.
- A good cluster name captures what its items have in common at a glance.
