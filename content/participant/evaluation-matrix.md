# Evaluation Matrix

You'll score a set of options against several criteria, **one criterion at a
time**.

- The screen shows one criterion (e.g. "Impact") and all the options.
- Give each option a rating on the scale shown.
- Tap **Next →** to move to the next criterion, and rate the options again.
- When you've done every criterion, tap **Done** to see the results matrix.

Your ratings save as you tap, so you can go back with **←** and adjust anything.
The Results tab shows the group's averages and a total for each option.
