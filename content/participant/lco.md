# LCO

LCO is a friendly way to react to an idea. For each one you add to three
sections:

- **Likes** — what you genuinely like about it.
- **Concerns** — what worries you, written as a question, e.g. "How to…?"
- **Opportunities** — new possibilities or directions the idea opens up.

Start with what you like. Concerns are welcome, but phrase them as questions so
they invite new thinking. The Opportunities you spot often become the next round
of ideas.
