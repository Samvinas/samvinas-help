# PMI

PMI looks at an idea from three directions before you judge it. For each idea add
to three sections:

- **Plus** — the good points.
- **Minus** — the weak or problematic points.
- **Interesting** — anything noteworthy or curious about it — neither good nor
  bad, but worth saying ("what if…?", "this reminds me of…").

The Interesting column is the useful one: it catches the things a simple
good/bad reaction would miss.
