# Timer — for facilitators

The Timer is a utility, not a flow tool: enable it once and a shared countdown
becomes available across every tool in your event, in the sticky footer at the
bottom of everyone's screen. Timeboxes are half of good facilitation — a
visible clock does the nagging so you don't have to.

## How to run it

- Enable the Timer in the **Utilities** section of your session view.
- Set a duration and start it. Everyone — participants, the display page, you —
  sees the same countdown in the footer.
- **Pause** and **Reset** are yours; Reset returns the clock to the duration
  you configured, ready to start again.
- The panel remembers your last duration, and you can type a custom number of
  minutes.

## Fullscreen presenter mode

Any device can turn the countdown into a full-screen clock — tap the footer
chip (or its ⛶ icon). Useful for a laptop feeding a projector, or a spare
tablet propped on a table. Fullscreen is a local view (each device chooses its
own), the screen stays awake, and your pause/reset controls are available in
the overlay.

## Facilitation tips

- Announce the timebox *before* starting the clock, and say what happens when
  it ends — "three minutes, then we vote" beats a countdown with no
  consequence.
- Short beats generous: a slightly-too-tight timebox keeps energy up, and you
  can always add a minute.
