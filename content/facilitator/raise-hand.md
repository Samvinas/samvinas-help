# Raise Hand — for facilitators

Raise Hand is a utility that gives the room a fair queue: participants tap ✋,
and you see who's waiting, in order, in the footer of your view. It replaces
the loudest-voice-wins dynamic with a visible, first-come queue — and it works
across whatever tool the group is currently using.

## How to run it

- Enable Raise Hand in the **Utilities** section of your session view.
- Participants tap the ✋ in their footer to join the queue (and tap again to
  leave it).
- Your footer shows the queue in order. **Next** clears the current speaker
  and moves on; **Clear All** empties the queue at a scene change.

## Facilitation tips

- Say how you'll use it, once: "if you want to speak, raise your hand in the
  app — I'll take people in order."
- Watch for hands that have waited long: a queue is only fair if it actually
  gets served.
- Clear the queue between activities so stale hands don't carry over.
