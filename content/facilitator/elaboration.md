# Elaboration — for facilitators

Elaboration is for depth: participants develop selected ideas with rich text and
sketches. Use it after you've narrowed the field, to turn shortlisted ideas into
fleshed-out proposals.

## How to run it

- Pipe in the shortlist (e.g. the top-voted ideas).
- Set a **Prompt** that guides the deepening ("What would it take to make this
  work?").

## Facilitation tips

- Let people choose which ideas to elaborate; **Sign Up** pairs well for
  ownership.
- Export the elaborations for follow-up after the session.
