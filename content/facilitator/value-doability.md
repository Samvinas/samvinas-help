# Value / Doability — for facilitators

A 2D scoring tool: participants rate each idea on two dimensions and see the
results as a scatter plot. Ideal for prioritising once you've generated and
grouped ideas.

## How to run it

- Pipe in the shortlist (often the top-voted ideas).
- Rename the axes in settings if "Value" and "Doability" aren't the right pair
  (e.g. Impact / Effort).
- Give people time — the Submit bar sits at the bottom of the list.

## Facilitation tips

- Discuss the **quadrants**, not individual dots: quick wins vs big bets vs
  not-now.
- Where agreement is low (the colouring shows it), dig in — people may be scoring
  on different assumptions.
