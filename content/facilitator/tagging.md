# Tagging — for facilitators

Tagging sorts piped items into facilitator-defined buckets (e.g.
short/medium/long-term, or by theme). It can run as a personal sort or as a group
activity that shows consensus.

## How to run it

- Pipe in the items and define your categories in settings.
- Choose single-user (personal sorting) or multi-user (group consensus) to fit
  the activity.

## Facilitation tips

- Keep categories few and clearly distinct.
- The consensus view highlights items the group sees differently — a good place
  to focus discussion.
