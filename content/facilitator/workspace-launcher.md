# Personal Workspace — for facilitators

Personal Workspace gives every participant their own copy of a mini-flow you
design — for individual reflection, drafting, or practice before coming back
together.

## How to run it

- Build the workspace template (the sequence of tools each person gets).
- Participants create and open their own; each is a real, private child event.

## Facilitation tips

- Great for "think alone, then share" patterns — individual work first, group
  convergence after.
- Use it when you want everyone to engage deeply, not just the vocal few.
