# Brain Writing — for facilitators

Brain Writing generates ideas silently and in parallel: each participant builds
on provocations drawn from others' ideas. It levels participation and avoids the
loudest-voice problem.

## How to run it

- Seed it with a stimulus (or pipe in starting ideas).
- Give a clear **Prompt** and a little time; the rounds do the rest.

## Facilitation tips

- Great when a few people dominate, or when a topic is sensitive and people think
  better in writing.
- Follow it with Clustering or Voting to make sense of the harvest.
