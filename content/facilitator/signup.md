# Sign Up — for facilitators

Sign Up turns a list into commitments: participants add their names to the items
they'll carry forward. A clean way to close a session with ownership.

## How to run it

- Pipe in the items (actions, tasks, ideas to develop).
- Frame the ask in the **Prompt** ("Which will you take the lead on?").

## Facilitation tips

- Read the sign-ups aloud to make commitments visible and real.
- Export the results so owners have a record after the session.
