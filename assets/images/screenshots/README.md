# Screenshots

Pictures of the Samvinas app itself, referenced from help pages as
`/assets/images/screenshots/<name>.png`.

These track the **product**, not your brand: update them when the app's UI
changes; you do not need to touch them when re-theming the site. (Org-specific
art — diagrams you might redraw in your own style — lives in
`../diagrams/` instead.)

House rules: PNG or WebP, capped at ~1200px wide, and every reference needs
alt text describing what the reader should take from the picture.
